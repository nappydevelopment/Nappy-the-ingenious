The Instruction

"Nappy the ingenious" is a brilliant Combination Artist.
He is able to guess a character of the Simpsons and solely with character-specific questions.

First gamemode Details
First, you think of a character from the universe of the television show The Simpsons. Then Nappy will ask you questions about your character that can answered with "yes", "no" or "I don't know".
If Nappy is sure he will dissolve and hopefully be right.
Second gamemode Details
For the second game mode you can turn the tables. This time Nappy will choose a character.
Now you need to guess this character. For this you have a list of questions to choose from.

Your goal should be to use as few questions as possible, because only then you can defeat Nappy.
The winner is the one who guessed the character right and needs the fewest questions.
Statistics Details
In statistics are the top 5 players listed. To appear in this list you have to beat Nappy.

Furthermore, the 5 most played characters are listed too.
Wiki Details
The wiki is holding information about every playable character. For quick search you have searching and filtering functions.
Settings Details
In the settings you have at the moment 3 options available.
You can choose between German and English
It is also possible to change the color scheme of the application.
Finally, you can specify that the second game mode is skipped (NOTE: This games aren't included in the statistics).
